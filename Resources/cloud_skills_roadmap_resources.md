## Notes on Using This Resource

- **Free vs Paid**: Most YouTube content is free; Udemy courses often go on sale for $9.99-$14.99
- **Hands-on Practice**: Always supplement video learning with actual implementation
- **Project Building**: Focus on the 3 main projects (RAG, Monitoring, CI/CD) for your portfolio
- **GitHub Repositories**: Clone and study the example repos, then build your own versions
- **Community Learning**: Join Discord/Slack communities for real-time help
- **Interview Prep**: Use the behavioral and technical interview resources in months 11-12
- **Certification Path**: Consider CKA (Kubernetes) or Terraform Associate after completing fundamentals

This roadmap is designed to take you from beginner to job-ready in 12 months with a mix of free and paid resources, focusing on practical skills and portfolio projects.
